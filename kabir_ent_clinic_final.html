About Dr. Shivani Patel

Dr. Shivani Patel (MS ENT) is a qualified otolaryngologist providing evidence-based medical and surgical care for a broad spectrum of ear, nose, throat, sinus, and head & neck conditions. Her clinical philosophy combines advanced expertise with transparent communication and individually tailored treatment.
